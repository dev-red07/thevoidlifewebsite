Community is at the heart of Outpost. Our coliving and coworking spaces are where the curious and adventurous go to meet, share ideas and travel together.

Live

Our spaces are designed to feel like home, featuring cosy rooms and private bathrooms, as well as relaxing communal areas to meet fellow Outposters and remote workers.

You’ll have reliable internet, regular cleaning services and staff onsite for support 24/7. All of our coliving spaces are located within vibrant neighborhoods, meaning it’s easy to get out there and explore on foot, by bike or scooter.

And best of all, if you stay 2 weeks or longer with us – you ’ll save more per night.

Work

We create inspiring cowork spaces where creators, entrepreneurs and digital nomads gather, meet and collaborate on their next big project together.

All of our cowork spaces have light-filled collaboration areas, focused air-con work zones, meeting rooms, workshop spaces, private offices and video call booths. We’ve got you covered with high-speed wi-fi, ergonomic chairs, adjustable desks and comfortable lounge areas.

Find your workspace

Experience

At Outpost events, you can mix and mingle with a diverse and engaging crew of people who match your vibe.

Join weekend trips to build genuine friendships with fellow digital nomads from all around the world. From mountain trekking to exploring local farms or vineyards, at Outpost we make sure your stay is one you will remember.
