Frequently asked questions

What’s included in a team retreat?

Accommodation and coworking memberships are included, always! We offer many additional services such as airport pickup, local SIM cards, meals, excursions and guided tours around our destinations. We can build packages according to your needs and wishes.

Do you have private spaces for working and taking meetings?

Absolutely, every Outpost coworking space has meeting rooms, private offices and video call booths, as well as hot desks and dedicated desk options. We also have whiteboards and projectors available on request.

How fast is the wifi speed in Outpost coworking spaces?

Our Bali workspaces have a 200mb/s shared ISP bandwidth and a dedicated 200mb/s line with different providers to ensure consistency even if one line goes out. In our Weligama property, we have 300mb/s shared ISP bandwidth with 100mb/s dedicated line and 200mb/s broadband with an on-site generator in case there are any power cuts. All of our coworking spaces use top of the line routers with industry-standard load balancing to provide additional stability for meetings, calls or streaming.

Can you help plan activities for our team?

Of course! We have extensive knowledge of Bali and we can help you create the perfect trip. How about a day trip to Nusa Penida or white water rafting in Ubud? Perhaps you’d like to catch the sunrise from an active volcano or see the dolphins in Lovina? We can arrange all of this and more for you and your team.

What is the price of a team retreat?

Get in touch for a personalized proposal. Just let us know how long you would like to stay, when you plan to arrive and how many people you want to bring along. Make sure to include if you want meals included and if you want us to suggest some activities for you and your team as well.
